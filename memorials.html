About MJ Granites Export

Established in 1995, MJ Granites Export has grown from a small local workshop to a globally recognized exporter of premium granite products. Based in Tamil Nadu, India, we combine generations of stone-working expertise with modern technology to create exceptional granite pieces that grace homes, temples, and public spaces worldwide.

Our 50,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility houses state-of-the-art cutting, polishing, and carving equipment alongside our team of master artisans who preserve traditional Indian stone-carving techniques.

Our Export Process

We've streamlined our export operations to ensure smooth international shipments of your granite products.

1

Product Selection & Quotation

Choose from our extensive product range or discuss custom requirements. We provide detailed quotes including product costs, packaging, and shipping estimates.

2

Order Confirmation

Once approved, we confirm the order and begin production. For custom pieces, we provide design approvals and material samples before full production.

3

Quality Inspection

Each piece undergoes rigorous quality checks. We provide inspection reports and photos before packaging to ensure complete satisfaction.

4

Export Packaging

Specialized packaging designed for international shipping. We use weather-resistant crates with shock-absorbent materials to protect your investment.

5

Shipping & Logistics

We handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and arrange shipping with reliable partners. Choose between sea or air freight options.

6

Delivery & Follow-up

Real-time tracking until delivery. Our team follows up to ensure complete satisfaction and assist with any installation or maintenance questions.
