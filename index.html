Our Export Process

From quarry to your destination - seamless international delivery

Material Selection

We source premium granite blocks directly from quarries, ensuring quality from the first step. Our team selects materials based on your project requirements and international standards.

Precision Cutting

Using advanced multi-blade cutting machines, we transform raw blocks into precise dimensions. Our cutting process minimizes waste and maximizes material utilization.

Surface Finishing

Each piece undergoes meticulous polishing, honing, or texturing based on specifications. We offer various finishes to meet different climate conditions and usage requirements.

Quality Inspection

Every product passes through our 12-point quality check system. We verify dimensions, surface quality, structural integrity, and aesthetic appeal before packaging.

Export Packaging

Specialized packaging designed for international shipping. We use shock-absorbent materials, moisture barriers, and reinforced wooden crates for safe ocean/air freight.

Global Logistics

We handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and coordinate with trusted freight partners. Real-time tracking provided until delivery at your destination.
